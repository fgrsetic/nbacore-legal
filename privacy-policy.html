Privacy Policy

App: Hoops'49  |  Last updated: June 23, 2026  |  Developer: Franjo Grsetic

This Privacy Policy describes how Hoops'49 ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application on Android and iOS.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication via Firebase Authentication.
  • Google account name and email — if you sign in with Google Sign-In.

We do not collect passwords directly. Authentication is handled entirely by Google Firebase.

Data You Create in the App

  • Favorite players — stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. Not uploaded to any server.
  • Onboarding preferences — stored locally on your device.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics events (e.g. which features are used, paywall interactions) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is attached to these events.

Purchase Information

If you subscribe to Hoops'49 PRO, the purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not receive or store your payment card details. Google's privacy policy applies to the transaction: policies.google.com/privacy.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services. All NBA data requests are routed through our backend; no personally identifiable information is forwarded to any data provider.

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — manages sign-in. Privacy policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics (Google) — collects anonymous crash reports and aggregated usage analytics. Privacy policy
  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app subscriptions on Android. Privacy policy
  • Apple StoreKit — handles in-app subscriptions on iOS. Privacy policy
  • API-Sports (NBA v2) — provides NBA player profiles, season statistics, standings, schedule, and game data. Documentation
  • BallDontLie — provides player injury status, confirmed lineups, contract data, and advanced statistics. Provider site
  • The Odds API — provides betting odds and player props data. Provider site
  • Upstash Redis — caches NBA data on our backend. No user-identifiable data is cached. Privacy policy
  • Fly.io — hosts our backend service. Privacy policy

NBA team logos displayed in the app are bundled with the app as static assets sourced from Wikipedia / Wikimedia Commons. They are loaded from local app storage and not fetched from any remote server, so no personal data is transmitted when logos are displayed.

Player avatars are rendered procedurally (programmatic jersey artwork) from team brand colors only. The app does not display, transmit, or store any player photographs or likenesses.

3. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app may send you a daily notification on days when your favorited players have games scheduled. Notifications are generated locally on your device using Android WorkManager. No notification data is sent to any external server.

4. Data Retention

  • Your Firebase account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Local app data (favorites, preferences) is stored on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

5. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. This will remove your authentication record from Firebase. Local data is erased when you uninstall the app.

To request manual deletion of any data, email us at: floppyspot1976@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

Hoops'49 is not directed at users under 21. We require all users to confirm they are 21 or older before accessing the app. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 21. If you believe a user under 21 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7.2 — Analytics events we record (updated 2026-06-23)

Hoops'49 records anonymized usage events to understand which features people use and to improve the product. We use Firebase Analytics, which processes data on Google's servers under Google's Privacy Policy.

Events we record

Navigation and engagement

  • Page views (Rankings, Players, Favorites, LeagueGame, Insights, Best Lines, Settings)
  • Compare opens, search queries (player names only — no free-form text)
  • Onboarding interest selections (e.g. "bets,fantasy")

Premium and subscription

  • Paywall views, premium CTA taps, premium feature unlocks
  • Subscription restore attempts

Projections and props (informational only)

  • Prop probability views (which stat, whether an EV pill was shown)
  • EV pill taps (which stat, direction over/under, EV sign positive/negative)
  • Game outcome views and card expansions (game ID, source surface)
  • Best Lines screen views, row taps (market, edge magnitude), filter changes, paywall shows

Bet tracking (your private bet log)

  • Bet added — we record: market type (e.g. "PTS"), direction (over/under), and a stake bucket (one of "<$10" / "$10-50" / "$50-200" / ">$200"). We never record the exact dollar amount you stake.
  • Bet deleted — whether it had been settled before deletion
  • Bet settled — outcome (WON / LOST / PUSH / VOID), market, direction
  • Bet tracker opened, paywall events, upgrade-CTA tapped
  • Background settle-worker aggregate counts (pending, settled, voided)

What we DON'T record

  • Your exact stake dollar amount (only quantized buckets)
  • Personally identifiable information beyond what you provide at sign-up (your email if you used email auth; we never sell this)
  • Your physical location, phone number, contacts, photos, or any device identifiers beyond Firebase's standard analytics ID

7.3 — Other Firebase services we use

  • Firebase Authentication — to verify your sign-in. Email + password or Google Sign-In. We store only what Firebase Auth stores: your UID, email, and last login timestamp.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — automatically receives crash reports when the app crashes. We do NOT attach your user ID, email, or any identifier to crash reports. Reports include only technical data (device model, OS version, stack trace).
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — used to deliver push notifications you've opted into. We do NOT use FCM for marketing.
