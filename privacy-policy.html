Privacy Policy

App: NBACore  |  Last updated: June 9, 2026  |  Developer: Franjo Grsetic

This Privacy Policy describes how NBACore ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication via Firebase Authentication.
  • Google account name and email — if you sign in with Google Sign-In.

We do not collect passwords directly. Authentication is handled entirely by Google Firebase.

Data You Create in the App

  • Favorite players — stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. Not uploaded to any server.
  • Onboarding preferences — stored locally on your device.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics events (e.g. which features are used, paywall interactions) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is attached to these events.

Purchase Information

If you subscribe to NBACore PRO, the purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not receive or store your payment card details. Google's privacy policy applies to the transaction: policies.google.com/privacy.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services. All NBA data requests are routed through our backend; no personally identifiable information is forwarded to any data provider.

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — manages sign-in. Privacy policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics (Google) — collects anonymous crash reports and aggregated usage analytics. Privacy policy
  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app subscriptions on Android. Privacy policy
  • Apple StoreKit — handles in-app subscriptions on iOS. Privacy policy
  • API-Sports (NBA v2) — provides NBA player profiles, season statistics, standings, schedule, and game data. Documentation
  • BallDontLie — provides player injury status, confirmed lineups, contract data, and advanced statistics. Provider site
  • The Odds API — provides betting odds and player props data. Provider site
  • Upstash Redis — caches NBA data on our backend. No user-identifiable data is cached. Privacy policy
  • Fly.io — hosts our backend service. Privacy policy

NBA team logos displayed in the app are bundled with the app as static assets sourced from Wikipedia / Wikimedia Commons. They are loaded from local app storage and not fetched from any remote server, so no personal data is transmitted when logos are displayed.

Player avatars are rendered procedurally (programmatic jersey artwork) from team brand colors only. The app does not display, transmit, or store any player photographs or likenesses.

3. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app may send you a daily notification on days when your favorited players have games scheduled. Notifications are generated locally on your device using Android WorkManager. No notification data is sent to any external server.

4. Data Retention

  • Your Firebase account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Local app data (favorites, preferences) is stored on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

5. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. This will remove your authentication record from Firebase. Local data is erased when you uninstall the app.

To request manual deletion of any data, email us at: floppyspot1976@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

NBACore is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
